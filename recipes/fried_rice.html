Fried Rice

Fried rice is a really simple recipe.

You can do it with whatever you have on your fridge

Ingredients

  • 200Gr of rice(better if it's one day old rice)
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Carrot
  • 2 Slices of ham
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat a frying pan and add the oil
  2. Beat the egg and add it on the pan when it's hot
  3. Add the rice and mix everything
  4. Add the ham and carrot cut in small cubes or slices
  5. Add salt to your liking
