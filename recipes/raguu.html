Raguu

A raguu is a tomato and meat based sauce.

Is usually used in recipes such as lasagna, pasta a la bolognesa and others.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of peeled tomatoes
  • 500Gr of ground beef
  • 500Gr of ground pork
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Celery
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. First cut the different vegetables in small cubes
  2. Heat the pot at medioum heat
  3. Add a couple tbs of ev olive oil
  4. Add both the ground beef and ground pork and season with some salt and black pepper
  5. Wait until the meat is golden and then add the vegetables with some salt
  6. When the onion starts to get a bit golden you can add the can of tomatos
  7. Add enough water so everything is immersed
  8. Decrease the heat to medium low and stir from time to time
  9. Let everything on the stove for 1h-2h
