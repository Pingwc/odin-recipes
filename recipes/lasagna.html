Lasagna

Simple recipe about italian lasagna.

The recipe is quite simple, requires only about 30min and a couple of ingredients


Ingredients

  • 5 Lasagna sheets
  • 1Kg Tomato sauce
  • 500Gr Ground meat
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Put 2 tbs of extra virgin olive oil on the frying pan
  2. Add the ground meat to the pan, sprinkle some salt and black pepper and wait until is golden
  3. Add the tomato sauce and stir everything slowly
  4. Let the different flavors to know each other for 40min in slow fire
  5. Meanwhile heat 1L of water
  6. Add the lasagna sheets to the water when bowling
  7. Add a layer of lasagna, then add the sauce with meat, then another sheet and so on
  8. Put the lasagna in the oven for 40min at 210ÂºC
